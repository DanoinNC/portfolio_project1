About Us

Serving the triangle area for over 30 years, whether you have come for the big game, food, drinks, company or all of the above, we have you covered.

We are the number one sports bar locally and offer a wide variety of food and drink selection. We have over 40 beers on tap and 10 120 inch large screen tv's to watch all of the big games.

We also offer specials through out the week including kids eat free Monday nights, military discount Wednesday and weekend drink specials to help you kick start the weekend right.
